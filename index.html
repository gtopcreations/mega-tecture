About Us

Based in Maitama in Abuja, Nigeria, MEGA-TECTURE CONSTRUCTIONS offers diverse range of real estate and property services including house and apartment construction, commercial and home renovations and remodelling, architectural design services, estate agency services, real estate development, estate management, analysis of real estate investment opportunities and implementation of projects.

The management team at MEGA-TECTURE CONSTRUCTIONS has over 15 years of combined experience in the Nigerian real estate marketplace with a particular emphasis on choice areas in Abuja such as Maitama , Wuse, Garki, Gwarimpa, Kuje, Gwagwalada and in Lagos State - Lekki, Ikoyi, Victoria Island, Oniru and Ikeja GRA.
